<p><p>The Powder In-Mould Coating (IMC) market focuses on two primary types: Sheet Moulding Compound (SMC) and Bulk Moulding Compound (BMC). SMC is a pre-impregnated composite that allows for lightweight, durable parts, primarily used in automotive and construction applications. BMC, on the other hand, is a thick, paste-like material ideal for complex shapes and high-strength products, commonly utilized in electrical and industrial sectors. Both types enhance surface finish and durability while supporting efficient manufacturing processes.</p></p>
