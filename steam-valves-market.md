<p><p>The steam valve market comprises three primary types: pneumatic, electric, and self-operated valves. Pneumatic steam valves utilize compressed air for operation, offering rapid response and precise control. Electric steam valves rely on electrical signals for actuation, providing automation and integration with control systems. Self-operated steam valves function without external energy sources, using the steam pressure for operation, which enhances reliability and reduces maintenance needs. Each type serves distinct applications in industrial processes, emphasizing efficiency and control in steam management.</p></p>
