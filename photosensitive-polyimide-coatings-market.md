<p><strong>The Photosensitive Polyimide Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photosensitive Polyimide</li><li>Negative Photosensitive Polyimide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photosensitive polyimide coatings market is segmented into positive and negative photosensitive polyimides. Positive photosensitive polyimides become soluble upon exposure to UV light, allowing for precise patterning in applications like microelectronics and semiconductor manufacturing. Conversely, negative photosensitive polyimides harden when exposed to UV light, making the unexposed areas soluble, which is beneficial for creating durable and intricate patterns. Both types are essential in the production of high-performance components, offering unique properties suitable for various technological applications.</p></p>
