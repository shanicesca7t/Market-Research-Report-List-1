<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floor-mounted radiography market primarily consists of two types: Computed Radiography (CR) Tech Digital X-Ray Systems and Digital Radiography (DR) Tech Digital X-Ray Systems. CR Tech systems utilize photostimulable phosphor plates to capture images, which are then digitized for review. In contrast, DR Tech systems convert X-ray signals directly into digital data, providing real-time imaging with higher efficiency and image quality. These technologies enhance diagnostic capabilities, streamline workflows, and improve patient outcomes in medical settings.</p></p>
