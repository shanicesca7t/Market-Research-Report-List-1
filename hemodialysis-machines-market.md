<p><ul><li>Single Pump Hemodialysis Machines</li><li>Double Pump Hemodialysis Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemodialysis machines market is primarily divided into two types: single pump and double pump hemodialysis machines. Single pump machines utilize a single pump for both blood and dialysate flow, offering simplicity and efficient space usage, making them ideal for home dialysis. In contrast, double pump machines employ separate pumps for blood and dialysate, enhancing control over flow rates and providing more precise treatment options. This type is often preferred in clinical settings for its versatility and improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
