<p><p>The neurodiagnostic and monitoring devices market encompasses various technologies essential for assessing brain and nerve function. Electroencephalogram (EEG) devices measure electrical activity in the brain, while electromyography (EMG) evaluates muscle response to nerve stimulation. Transcranial Doppler assesses blood flow in the brain, crucial for stroke detection. Intracranial pressure monitors measure pressure within the skull, aiding in critical care, and cerebral oximeters track cerebral oxygen levels, helping manage neurological conditions. Together, these devices play a vital role in diagnosing and monitoring neurological health.</p></p>
