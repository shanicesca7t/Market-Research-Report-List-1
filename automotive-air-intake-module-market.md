<p><strong>The Automotive Air Intake Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Air Type</li><li>Ram Air Type</li><li>Short Ram Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air intake module market consists of three main types: cold air type, ram air type, and short ram type. Cold air intakes are designed to draw in cooler air from outside, enhancing engine performance and efficiency. Ram air intakes utilize forward motion to force air into the engine, boosting power at higher speeds. Short ram intakes offer a simpler design, improving throttle response by reducing airflow resistance but may draw in warmer air. Each type serves distinct performance and aesthetic preferences for vehicles.</p></p>
